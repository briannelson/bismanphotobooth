No Prints

Why don't we offer prints?

  • It's cheaper No prints means no ink, no paper, no printer. And that allows us to be a lot cheaper than our competitors.
  • It's quicker With all the time you'll save not waiting for prints, you'll be able to take more pictures — and that's the fun part.
  • It's easier Less moving parts means less can go wrong. And we don't have to stop to reload the ink or the paper.
  • Most people don't care We've found that most people either lose or forget those little strips they get at other photo booths.

Just because you can't give out prints that night doesn't mean you have no way to spread the photo love. You can share your online gallery with your guests and they can print their own photos, or you can print photos of your guests and include them in your thank you cards for a personal touch.
